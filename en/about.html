公司介绍 Company Profile

Shanghai Zi'Ai Industrial Co., Ltd.成立于2014年7月，注册资本100万元。

公司是一家专注于工业与科技领域的技术服务与产品销售型企业。核心团队由数名在IT软硬件及工业产品领域拥有深厚经验的资深工程师组成。

Founded in July 2014, Shanghai Zi'Ai Industrial Co., Ltd. is a technology services and product sales company focused on industrial and technology sectors. Our core team consists of experienced senior engineers in IT hardware/software and industrial products.
